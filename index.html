A comprehensive, four day long camp designed to teach the ins and outs of public forum debate.

Why, what, and who is DMV Debate?

Our camp has a proven success rate, with over 90% of our students going on to debate for their school team. Our instructors, Raunak and Rishi, are Varsity Public Forum Debaters at Thomas Jefferson High School. They have both debated since middle school, placing at numerous regional WACFL tournaments. DMV Debate teaches the basics of public forum debate and public speaking. The camp teaches rising 6th to rising 9th graders in Herndon, VA.
