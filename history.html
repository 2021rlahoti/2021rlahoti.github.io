Charitable Outreach

DMV Debate Camp serves the community as a proud non-profit. Over the past two years, we've worked with local organizations to make a difference for the community.

After conducting two succesful sessions of camp during the Summer of 2019, DMV Debate Camp reached out to the Fairfax County Animal Shelter to work on a community outreach program. The camp worked with the shelter to donate supplies and funds towards helping animals at the shelter. We donated just under $1,000 of materials to help the animals.
